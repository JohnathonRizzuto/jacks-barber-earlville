Our Story

The Chair on South Ottawa.

Jack's Barber Shop has been Earlville's hometown chair for over two decades. We're a small-town barber shop in the truest sense — clean cut, honest work, fair prices, and a conversation that's worth the seat as much as the cut itself.

First-time visitors and lifelong regulars get the same care. Whether you need a clean fade, a classic taper, or just a tidy-up before church on Sunday, we've got the chair waiting.

"Cut sharp, talk fair, send 'em out smiling."
